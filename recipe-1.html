Maggi

Maggi noodles recipe | maggi masala recipe 4 ways | cheese maggi & vegetable maggi with detailed photo and video recipe. perhaps one of the popular and easy instant snack meal recipes prepared with maggi noodles and tastemakers. it is simple, easy and more important attracts almost all types of audience with no age bar. in addition the noodles recipe was targeted just for morning breakfast, but it can be easily prepared for any meal including lunch, dinner and even evening snacks.

Preperation Time

5 min.

Cook Time

5 min.

Serving

1 serving

Recipes Breakfast Healthy Fried Dessert Dinner

Steps

step 1

Firstly, in pan heat 2 tsp oil and saute 2 clove garlic and ½ onion.

step 2

add ½ carrot, 2 tbsp capsicum, ½ tomato and 2 tbsp sweet corn.

step 3

saute until the vegetables turn crunchy.

step 4

further add ¼ tsp turmeric, ½ tsp chilli powder, ¼ tsp salt and saute until the spices turn aromatic.

step 5

add 1 cup water and get to a boil.

step 6

now bread 1 pack maggi noodles roughly and add 1 pack tastemaker.

step 7

mix well and boil for 2 minutes or until the noodles are cooked well.

step 8

finally, add 2 tbsp coriander and enjoy masaledar maggi.

Ingredients

2 tsp oil ½ onion, finely chopped ½ carrot, finely chopped 2 tbsp capsicum, chopped ½ tomato, chopped 2 tbsp sweet corn ¼ tsp turmeric ½ tsp chilli powder ¼ tsp salt 1 cup water 1 pack maggi noodles 1 pack tastemaker 2 tbsp coriander, finely chopped

Tools

Knife

Pan

vegetables

Maggie masala
